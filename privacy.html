Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you enroll in our course, we collect the following information:

  • Personal details: Your name, email address, and phone number provided during enrollment.
  • Payment information: Your UPI Transaction ID / UTR number for verification purposes.
  • Payment screenshot: An image of your payment confirmation that you upload during the enrollment process.
  • Communication data: Messages exchanged on Telegram for support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Verify your payment and grant course access on Telegram.
  • Provide 24×7 customer support through our Telegram channels.
  • Send course-related updates and important announcements.
  • Prevent fraudulent enrollments and protect the integrity of our service.

3. Payment Data

All payments are processed directly through UPI. We do not collect, store, or have access to your bank account details, card numbers, or UPI PIN. Payment verification is done manually using only the Transaction ID and screenshot you provide.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. Your data stays strictly within our team and is used only for course delivery and support purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your enrollment information for the duration of your course access and as required by applicable regulations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us on Telegram.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us on Telegram for any such requests and we will respond promptly.
